VOLTAGE VERIFICATION POINTS:
----------------------------
Measure from Wire 0 (Black) to:
  Wire 1:  ~3.6-4.2V   (1S)
  Wire 2:  ~7.2-8.4V   (2S)
  Wire 3:  ~10.8-12.6V (3S)
  ...
  Wire 14: ~50.4-58.8V (14S) ← Total Pack Voltage


CURRENT FLOW:
-------------
Discharge Path:
  Battery NEG → BMS B- → BMS FET → BMS P- → XT90 NEG → Load
  Battery POS → XT90 POS → Load

Charge Path:
  Charger NEG → XT90 NEG → BMS P- → BMS Protection → BMS B- → Battery NEG
  Charger POS → XT90 POS → Battery POS
